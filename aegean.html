AEGEAN
ISLANDS

Cyclades, Dodecanese, Sporades, North Aegean, Crete

Some of the most beautiful islands in the world are found here. Thousands of islands are spread out throughout the Aegean Sea reaching all the way to the shores of Turkey. Listed below is an island for everyone to enjoy.

Santorini

Located in the Cyclades, Santorini is a volcanic island with breathtaking sunsets and beautiful views, perfect for romantic travelers. Black sand beaches and scenic scooter rides, along with postcard towns are found here.

Things to See

  • Sunset in Oia
  • See Real Atlantis
  • Hike Volcano
  • Akrotiri Lighthouse
  • Amoudi Bay
  • Kamari Beach
  • Three Bells of Fira
  • Perissa Black Sand Beach

Mykonos

If you want to party all night until the sun comes up, and keep going some more, then Mykonos is perfect for you. One of the smallest islands found in the Cyclades, it has a reputation for party goers and beautiful scenery.

Things to See

  • Agrari Beach
  • Paradise Beach
  • Super Paradise Beach
  • Little Venice
Back to the Top

Rhodes

Located just off the southwestern coast of Turkey you will find one of the bigger Greek islands, and the biggest in the dodecanese. Rhodes is known for its sunny days and ancient sites. Extremely popular with tourists, it is a great island to visit, but can be very busy during peak travel season.

Things to See

  • Palace of Grand Master
  • Acropolis of Lindos
  • Monastery of Filerimos
  • Acropolis of Rhodes
  • Monolithos Castle
  • Traganou Beach

Lesbos

Just off the west coast of Turkey is the Greek island of Lesbos. It is the birthplace of numerous poets and is the third largest islands in Greece, after Crete and Evia. Famous for ouzo, Lesbos has a great tradition and rich history.

Things to See

  • Castle of Molyvos
  • Natural Museum
  • Skala Eressos Beach
  • Castle of Mytilene
  • Ouzo Museum

Paros

One of the most beautiful islands in Greece, and the world, Paros is known for its charming villages, beautiful beaches, and fine white marble.

Things to See/Do

  • Naoussa
  • Kolymbithres Beach
  • Parikia Village
  • Lefkes
  • Lageri Beach
